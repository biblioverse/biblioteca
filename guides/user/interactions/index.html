      Book interaction

      You can mark any book as “Read”, “Don’t want to read” or “In my reading List” by using the buttons below the book cover on the book’s page.

      Books that you have marked as “Read” will appear in your reading timeline and bey greyed out in the lists. You can access your reading timeline by clicking on the “Reading timeline” link in the navigation bar.

      Books that you have marked as “Don’t want to read” will be hidden from the lists and will not appear in your reading timeline.

      Books that you have marked as “In my reading List” will appear on your homepage.
