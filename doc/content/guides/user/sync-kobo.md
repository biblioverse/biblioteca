---
title: Sync Kobo Devices
---

It is possible to sync your Kobo devices with Biblioteca.

{{% steps %}}

### Create a static shelf

Create a new [static shelf](/biblioteca/guides/user/shelf) on your profile. And add some books in it.

### Create a new Kobo device

Go to the "My Profile" page on the Kobo Settings tab and click on the "Create new Kobo device" button.

### Follow the configuration instructions

Follow the instructions on the kobo device page in biblioteca.

You will need to plug your kobo device to your computer and edit a configuration file.
The lines to edit and the values to enter are detailed in the app.

### Sync the Kobo device

Once the configuration file is edited, unmount your kobo device and click on the "Sync" button on the kobo device.

### Read and sync again

Read your books on the Kobo device and sync it again to update your progress on Biblioteca.

{{% /steps %}}
